Pfizer Software Hackathon

September 13, 2024

Summary

In late August, I found out about the inaugural Pfizer Software Hackathon through a Society of Women Engineers email blast. I had never participated As a proponent of equity at all levels, I was intrigued. Not only did this give me the chance to learn more about a potential future in the biomedical industry, but it addressed the same issues I held dearest.
